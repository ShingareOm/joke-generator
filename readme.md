### Joke-Generator
<img title="Project-Image" alt="" src="/projectimage.png">

Joke Generator is a website that generates jokes based on the tags selected by the user. You can choose from a variety of topics, such as animals, politics, sports, technology, etc. and get a random joke that matches your preferences. You can also rate the jokes and share them with your friends.

Joke Generator supports multiple languages, such as English, Hindi, Marathi, etc. You can switch the language from the settings menu and enjoy jokes in your preferred language.

## How to use

To use Joke Generator, follow these steps:

- Visit the website at [Github Profile](https://github.com/ShingareOm/joke-generator.git)
- Select the tags that you want to include in your joke from the list on the left side of the screen. You can select multiple tags or none at all.
- Click on the "Generate Joke" button on the right side of the screen and wait for a few seconds.
- A joke will appear on the screen. You can read it and laugh or groan as you wish.
- You can rate the joke by clicking on the stars below it. You can also share the joke with your friends by clicking on the social media icons below it.
- To generate another joke, click on the "Generate Joke" button again or change the tags as you like.

## Features

Joke Generator has the following features:

- A large database of jokes from various sources and categories
- A user-friendly interface that allows easy selection of tags and generation of jokes
- A multilingual support that enables jokes in different languages
- A rating system that lets users provide feedback on the jokes
- A sharing system that lets users spread the laughter with their friends
